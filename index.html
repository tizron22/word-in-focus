How to play Word In Focus

Firstly select your difficulty (Default at Medium).

You must guess the word in the gaming tiles, once you have a valid word click the Enter Button to submit.

After each guess, the tile will change colour to how close your guess was and if you guess correctly you go to the next round.

Each round completed you'll obtain points, to get the most points guess the words in the lease amount of guesses.

Difficulty Points Guesses
Hard 25 5
Medium 15 6
Easy 5 7

Your points for that round are divided against the guesses you took.

Example: If you selected Hard difficulty and guessed the word on the second attempt, you would have 12.5 points for that round.
